Follow these steps to properly treat sprains and strains:

  1. Rest the injured area to prevent further damage.
  2. Apply ice to the affected area for 20-30 minutes every few hours to reduce swelling.
  3. Compress the area with an elastic bandage to help control swelling, but not too tight.
  4. Elevate the injured area above heart level to reduce swelling.
  5. Avoid putting weight on the injured area and use crutches or a brace if necessary.
  6. If the pain or swelling persists, or if you suspect a more severe injury, consult a doctor.

Always consult a healthcare professional if the injury seems severe or if you're unsure of the proper treatment.
