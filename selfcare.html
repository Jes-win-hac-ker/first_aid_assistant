Self-Care Tips

💧 Dehydration

Drink water regularly, especially in hot weather or during physical activity.

😴 Fatigue

Get adequate sleep and take short breaks during work or study.

🤕 Mild Headache

Rest in a quiet place, hydrate, and use cold compress if needed.

🧼 Skin Irritation

Clean gently, avoid scratching, and apply soothing lotion or aloe vera.
