Follow these steps to properly treat a cut:

  1. Clean the wound with water and mild soap. Avoid using alcohol or hydrogen peroxide as they can damage tissue.
  2. Stop any bleeding by applying gentle pressure with a clean cloth or bandage.
  3. If necessary, apply an antiseptic to reduce the risk of infection.
  4. Cover the wound with a sterile bandage or gauze pad to keep it clean and protected.
  5. Change the bandage daily and monitor for signs of infection, such as redness or swelling.
  6. If the cut is deep, large, or doesn't stop bleeding, seek medical attention immediately.

Always seek professional help for severe cuts or if you're unsure how to treat the wound.
