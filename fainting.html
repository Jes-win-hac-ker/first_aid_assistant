Instructions for treating fainting:

  • Lay the person down on their back in a safe position.
  • Raise their legs above the level of their heart to improve blood flow to the brain.
  • Loosen any tight clothing and make sure they are comfortable.
  • Ensure they are breathing normally.
  • If they don't regain consciousness in a few minutes, seek medical help immediately.
