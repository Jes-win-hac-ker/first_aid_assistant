Follow these steps to properly treat a nosebleed:

  1. Stay calm and sit upright. Avoid lying down, as this can increase bleeding.
  2. Pinch your nostrils together with your thumb and index finger to stop the bleeding.
  3. Lean forward slightly to prevent blood from flowing into your throat.
  4. Breathe through your mouth while pinching your nose for at least 10-15 minutes.
  5. If the bleeding continues, try applying a cold compress to the back of your neck or the nose area.
  6. If the nosebleed doesn't stop after 20 minutes, seek medical help.

Always consult a healthcare professional if the nosebleed is frequent or severe.
