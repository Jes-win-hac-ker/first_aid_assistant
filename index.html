First Aid Assistant

🩸 Bleeding

Apply firm pressure and elevate the wound.

😷 Choking

Back blows and abdominal thrusts may be needed immediately.

😵 Fainting

Lay the person down and elevate legs. Ensure they're breathing.

🌡️ Heatstroke

Move to cool place, hydrate, and reduce body temperature quickly.

✂️ Cuts

Clean wound, apply ointment, and bandage.

🦶 Sprains & Strains

Use rest, ice, compression, elevation (R.I.C.E).

🔔 Nosebleed

Pinch nose and lean forward to prevent swallowing blood.

🧠 Minor Head Injury

Apply cold pack, observe for dizziness, monitor symptoms.

Simple remedies you can manage by yourself at home.
