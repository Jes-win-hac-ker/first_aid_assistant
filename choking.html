ü§ê Choking

Instructions for treating choking:

  • If the person is coughing, encourage them to keep coughing.
  • If the person can't breathe or speak, give back blows:
    • Position yourself behind the person.
    • Bend them forward and deliver 5 back blows between the shoulder blades with the heel of your hand.
  • If back blows don't work, give abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver):
    • Stand behind the person and place a fist above their navel.
    • Grab your fist with your other hand and perform quick, inward and upward thrusts.
  • If the person becomes unconscious, start CPR and call emergency services immediately.
