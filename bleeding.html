Instructions for treating bleeding:

  • Apply firm pressure to the wound using a clean cloth or bandage.
  • If possible, elevate the wounded area above the heart level to reduce blood flow.
  • If bleeding does not stop, apply more pressure and call for emergency medical assistance.
  • In case of severe bleeding, seek immediate medical attention by calling an ambulance.
